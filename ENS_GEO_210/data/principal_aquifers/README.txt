This folder contains shapefiles used in the Map of the Principal Aquifers of the United States
as presented at: https://water.usgs.gov/ogw/aquifer/map.html

The shapefile 'aquifrp025.shp' contains the data for the 'Principal Aquifers of the United States'

The shapefile 'alvaqfp025.shp' contains the data for the 'Aquifers of the Alluvial and Glacial Origin'
